Why We Do What We Do

Climate Change is a growing problem within our communities.Urban cities in Arizona like Pheonix are becoming more poluted.Wanting to heal the environment is the reason for Nature's Plan.Founders Joaquin Santiago and Stevan Guadalupe Plascencia-Gutierrez wanted to help make the community air cleaner while also making it more visually pleasing.

What We Do

Donate today to help Sponsor change in your community today.Nature's Plan plants 2 trees every week to help clean the air in Arizona.Buy a personal Potted Plant to help out and also liven up the home or workplace.Buy some T-Shirts,Long Sleeves, or Hoodies to show your support in the fight against air pollution.
